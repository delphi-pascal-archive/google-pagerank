Introduction

Yes! Now Delphi developers can embed Google PageRank retrieving functionality
into own applications. This components allows you to easily retrieve Google's
PageRank value and also show it by manner similar to Google Toolbar.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Requirements

 - Borland Delphi 5,6,7,2005 or 2006.
